Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Overview

KeyVibe is a typing practice and multiplayer typing game. We collect limited information needed to run the app, support login, save profile settings, and show gameplay progress.

Information We May Collect

When you sign in, we may process your Google account email address, display name, and profile image. We also store gameplay-related data such as typing scores, best WPM, test counts, selected settings, unlocked badges, and profile preferences.

How We Use Information

We use this information to authenticate your account, personalize your profile, enable multiplayer identity, maintain leaderboards, and improve the app experience.

Advertising

KeyVibe may display ads through third-party advertising partners such as Google AdSense. These providers may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads according to their policies and applicable laws.

Third-Party Services

KeyVibe uses services such as Firebase, Supabase, Vercel, and Google AdSense. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Cookies and Local Storage

KeyVibe may use browser storage and related technologies to remember settings such as theme, sound preferences, selected language, and local gameplay state.

Children's Privacy

KeyVibe is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law to consent to data processing on their own.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Contact page.
